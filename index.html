FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to complete a PropID report?
For homeowners, it typically takes 15–25 minutes from opening the invite link to submitting. The AI analysis and PDF are usually ready within an hour after submission.
Is my data private?
Yes. All photos and information are stored encrypted with strict access controls. Reports are only shared with parties you specifically authorize. We never sell or rent personal data. The public verify page only shows the city and postal code — never the full address or value.
How accurate is the AI valuation?
The AI provides an estimated value range with a confidence level (high, medium, low) based on real comparable sales found via live web search at the time of analysis. It's a market-context estimate, not a formal appraisal — always pair with a licensed appraiser for legal use.
How does the condition rating work?
A vision pass on every photo gives the property one of six tiers — excellent, very good, good, average, poor, or very poor — each with a corresponding adjustment percentage applied to the AI's raw market estimate. The reasoning behind the rating is printed on the valuation page.
Do I need to download an app?
No. PropID runs entirely in your phone's browser. Just open your invite link and you're ready to start.
What if my GPS location is wrong?
PropID detects mismatches between your GPS and the entered address automatically and flags them for your broker to review. You can also manually search for the correct address.
Can I update photos after submitting?
If your broker requests retakes for specific rooms, you'll get a new link to update only those photos. Otherwise, photos are locked once submitted to maintain the audit trail.
Who pays for a PropID report?
PropID is currently in early access. Pricing varies depending on your broker, lender, or insurer arrangement. Contact the party who sent you the invite for specifics.
Where is PropID available?
PropID is rolling out across Canadian markets. Sign up for early access and we'll let you know when we launch in your area.
