The homeowner experience

Done from your
driveway in minutes

1

Open your invite link

No app to install. Works in any mobile browser. Your personal invite is pre-filled with your property details.

2

Confirm GPS at the property

PropID verifies you're physically at the address. Photos can only be taken once GPS is confirmed — no remote submissions.

Guided room-by-room prompts walk you through the home. Each photo is GPS-stamped and securely uploaded.
